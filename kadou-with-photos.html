Amanda Theron · Owner
The face behind Kadou

Law degree. Flower heart.

Born between the Northern Suburbs of Cape Town and the beautiful Ceres Karoo, Amanda passed her law admission exams — including the specialised conveyancing exam. Then the heart said otherwise. She listened.

Kadou is the Afrikaans rendering of the French cadeau — meaning gift. The name was divinely inspired. Through over a decade of weddings, Amanda has come to realise she is the daily receiver of that gift: a platform for love, expression, and beauty she would not trade for anything.

Based in Stellenbosch · Available throughout South Africa · Destination weddings welcome.
